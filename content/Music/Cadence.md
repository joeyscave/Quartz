Chord progression of at least 2 chords that ends a phrase, section, or piece of music, creating a sense of full or partial resolution.

### Finished cadences
Always end on chord I

##### Perfect/Authentic
- V – I

##### Plagal “Amen”
- IV – I

### Unfinished cadences
Never end on chord I

##### Imperfect/Half
- I/II/IV – V

##### Interrupted/Deceptive
- V – VI
